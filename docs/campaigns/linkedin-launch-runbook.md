# LinkedIn Launch Runbook

Purpose: execute a disciplined 5-week OPLURIX LinkedIn launch without having
to rediscover the cadence, CTA logic, or posting rules each week.

This runbook keeps the best structure from the external campaign files, but
updates the CTA logic to match the current repo and funnel.

## 1. Campaign Role

The campaign should do three things in sequence:

1. establish authority
2. build ethical trust
3. convert the right people into:
   - checklist signups
   - product-page visitors
   - direct buyers only when the audience is warm enough

## 2. Master Rules

- Links never in the LinkedIn post body
- First comment within 5 minutes
- Post only when you can engage in the first hour
- EN and FR versions on different days
- No weekend posting
- 3 to 5 hashtags max
- Sunday batch session remains the content-prep anchor

## 3. Recommended Time Windows

Use the cadence from the external files unless data proves otherwise:

- Tuesday 13:00 EAT
- Wednesday 16:00 EAT
- Thursday 13:00 EAT
- Friday 16:00 EAT

## 4. Weekly Rhythm

- Tuesday: thought leadership
- Wednesday: proof / method / demo
- Thursday: educational
- Friday: founder story or conversion

## 5. Launch Logic By Week

### Week 1

Goal:

- establish credibility
- no hard product push

### Week 2

Goal:

- deepen trust
- show real scientific grounding

### Week 3

Goal:

- teach the ethics/process problem
- capture checklist leads

### Week 4

Goal:

- introduce the solution softly

### Week 5

Goal:

- convert with more direct offer language

## 7. First-Comment Routing Rules

### Cold posts

Use no link or a soft route:

- homepage
- about page
- projects page

Best fit:

- Posts 1, 2, 3, 5, 8

### Checklist capture posts

Use checklist route:

- Post 4 EN -> checklist EN
- Post 4 FR -> checklist FR

### Warm solution posts

Use product page route:

- Post 6 EN/FR
- Post 7 EN/FR
- Post 9 EN/FR

### Hot conversion posts

Use:

- direct checkout if the post is explicitly sales-forward
- product page if you want one more layer of warming

Best fit:

- Post 10 EN/FR

## 9. Weekly Operating SOP

### Sunday batch session

1. Prepare this week's 4 posts
2. Confirm visuals
3. Confirm first-comment links
4. Confirm the first-hour availability windows
5. Identify 5 to 10 target accounts to engage with

### Posting day

1. Publish on time
2. Add first comment within 5 minutes
3. Stay active for 60 minutes
4. Reply to comments with substance

### Saturday review

Log:

- best post by comments
- best post by profile visits if visible
- checklist signups
- product clicks
- direct buyer signals

## 10. What To Keep From The External Campaign Files

Keep:

- the discipline
- the educational sequencing
- the science-grounded tone
- the bilingual rhythm

## 11. What To Override From The External Campaign Files

Override:

- any instruction that pushes Product 1 too early under pure value posts
- any instruction that assumes all 10 products are going live immediately
- any direct link habit that ignores the current temperature-based routing

## 12. Done Means

- each post has a clear role
- each first comment has the right route
- no cold audience is forced straight into checkout
- the launch becomes a measured sequence, not random posting
