What OPLURIX is now

A live product layer

OPLURIX turns recurring conservation communication bottlenecks into bounded products. The current live offer is the Expert-to-Influencer Content Engine, built for researchers, NGO staff, and field professionals who need a credible public voice.

A research visibility layer

The ATBC 2026 campaign shows how real field work moves into public visibility, collaboration, and support. It is proof that OPLURIX is tied to actual conservation work, not just product packaging.

A practical support layer

The services line gives conservation teams a bounded way to get help with cleanup, validation, and data usability without pretending every need requires a large consulting build.

What holds it together

  • Field-grounded work in Madagascar, not abstract positioning.
  • Scientific honesty before hype or empty “impact” language.
  • Useful systems that help conservation knowledge travel further.
  • Mission-first public communication that still makes room for revenue.

What OPLURIX is not

  • Not generic social-media advice painted green.
  • Not a fake large catalog pretending everything is already mature.
  • Not a black-box conservation tech promise detached from field reality.
  • Not a substitute for the longer-term ERIS mission, but one way of feeding it.

Why products, research, and services all belong here

OPLURIX is intentionally not just a storefront and not just a research diary. The products create practical value and revenue. The research pages create trust and visibility. The services line creates a path for bounded collaboration with real teams. Together, they make the public mission legible without pretending the platform is more complete than it is.

That same mix also supports the longer arc. Every product, proof page, or service delivery can strengthen the credibility needed for ERIS to become more than an idea: a serious, stewardship-first ecological intelligence direction grounded in present-day work.
