# Independent Biodiversity Data Support

This page describes the independent, non-Techni service line built around
biodiversity data cleaning, validation, and reporting support.

## What This Helps With

Many conservation teams do the hard fieldwork first and only later discover
that their spreadsheets, GPS fields, species names, dates, or habitat labels
need cleanup before analysis, mapping, or reporting can move forward.

This service is designed for that stage.

## Who This Is For

- biodiversity NGOs
- field-research teams
- conservation programmes with survey data
- donor-reporting or MRV-adjacent teams that need cleaner tables

## Core Services

### Free 5-20 row sample review

A short review of a small extract to show what would need correction,
standardization, or restructuring before a full cleanup quote.

### Dataset cleaning and validation

Support for biodiversity datasets that need:

- duplicate removal
- date normalization
- species-name harmonization
- missing-value handling
- field standardization
- audit-style correction notes

### GPS and taxonomy validation pass

A focused review for records with suspect coordinates, naming inconsistencies,
or map-preparation issues.

### Reporting summary pack

A short support layer for teams that need a cleaner table plus a concise note
explaining what was corrected and what still needs checking.
