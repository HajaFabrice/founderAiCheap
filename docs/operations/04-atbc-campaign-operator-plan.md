# ATBC Campaign Operator Plan

Purpose: make the ATBC campaign reusable and measurable without mixing missions.

## Core campaign logic

Every campaign touchpoint should support one of three actions:

1. read the research story
2. support the travel/research directly
3. buy the product that supports the work

Do not blur those together in one CTA sentence.

## Weekly campaign rhythm

### Version A. Research-first week

1. LinkedIn long-form post
2. shorter LinkedIn follow-up
3. Facebook page version
4. direct message / WhatsApp version

### Version B. Product-support week

1. product angle post
2. research proof callback
3. CTA to Payhip or product page

## Channel rules

### LinkedIn

- best for full story
- use the ATBC page link
- use the dedicated share preview

### Facebook

- shorter and more visual
- keep donation and product support clearly separate

### WhatsApp

- concise
- one clear link
- best for warm contacts

## Routing rules

- cold research interest -> ATBC page
- direct support intent -> PayPal support link
- mission-aligned buyer intent -> live product page or checkout

## Asset rules

Use:

- ATBC preview image for ATBC page shares
- general site preview for broader OPLURIX shares

## Minimum campaign log to keep

For each push, note:

- date
- language
- channel
- asset used
- link used
- objective

## Done means

- campaign posts do not improvise the same message repeatedly
- support paths stay clean
- you can tell which channel is helping with visibility or support
