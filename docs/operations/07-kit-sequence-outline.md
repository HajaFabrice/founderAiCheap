# Kit Sequence Outline

Purpose: keep the first email logic simple and aligned with the OPLURIX funnel.

This is not the full copy deck. It is the structure we should implement first.

## Sequence A. Checklist EN

### Email 1. Deliver the checklist

Goal:

- deliver value immediately
- set expectations
- position the full paid system lightly

Include:

- printable checklist link
- one sentence on why the checklist exists
- one sentence on the live product as next step

### Email 2. Why ethical communication matters

Goal:

- build trust
- deepen the logic behind the checklist

Include:

- short founder/research-grounded context
- one concrete cautionary example

### Email 3. One useful communication insight

Goal:

- help before selling

Include:

- one small framework or lesson
- one practical example

### Email 4. Introduce the live product directly

Goal:

- show why the full product exists

Include:

- before/after problem
- what the paid system helps do
- link to product page or checkout depending on traffic temperature

### Email 5. Action invitation

Goal:

- invite next step without hype

Options:

- buy the live product
- reply with a question
- stay on updates

## Sequence B. Checklist FR

Use the same structure with:

- a natural French tone
- FR checklist asset
- FR product page where appropriate

## Sequence C. Product interest

Keep much shorter.

### Email 1

- acknowledgement
- direct next step
- link to live product

### Email 2

- proof
- what happens after purchase

### Email 3

- soft close or invitation to reply

## Rule

Do not overbuild the first version.

The first working sequence is more valuable than the perfect future sequence.
