Services in English

Practical data cleanup for biodiversity teams that need usable outputs fast.

This is a narrow, useful service line for conservation datasets that are almost ready, but not yet clean enough for analysis, mapping, or donor reporting.

Best-fit situations

Field survey spreadsheets

Species observations, GPS fields, habitat labels, dates, and mixed-format entries that need standardization.

Camera-trap or monitoring exports

Tables that need cleaning before they can be summarized, mapped, or passed into analysis.

Donor or programme reporting pressure

Teams that need a cleaner dataset plus a short note explaining what was corrected and what still needs review.

MRV-adjacent biodiversity data work

Practical support for traceable data preparation without pretending the work is a full software platform.
