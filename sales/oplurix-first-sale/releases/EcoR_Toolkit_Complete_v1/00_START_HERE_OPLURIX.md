# EcoR Toolkit Complete v1

Thank you for purchasing `EcoR Toolkit Complete`.

This package is built for:
- ecology and biology graduate students
- early-career researchers
- field biologists
- conservation professionals working with biodiversity count data

## What You Get

- annotated R scripts
- Claude prompts for debugging and interpretation
- field data templates
- ggplot2 figure templates
- workflow guides for moving from field notes to Results-ready outputs

## Open These First

1. `README.md`
2. `01_Start_Here/Quick_Start_Guide.md`
3. `05_Workflow_Guides/From_Field_Notes_to_Results_Section.md`

## What This Product Does Not Replace

- statistical judgment
- supervision from a PI or project lead
- project-specific ecological interpretation
- final responsibility for data quality and scientific claims
