# Private Community Access

Pro buyers are invited to a private WhatsApp community.

## Suggested Setup

1. Create one private WhatsApp group for Pro buyers only.
2. Send the invite manually after payment confirmation, not as a public link on the storefront.
3. Keep the group focused on EcoR workflow questions, biodiversity data, field methods, and scientific writing.
4. Remove buyers manually if the group drifts away from the support boundary.

## Founder Delivery Rule

- Do not publish the invite link on the public site.
- Send the invite link manually in the Pro delivery email.
- Record `whatsapp_invite_sent_at` and `whatsapp_joined_at` in the Pro support tracker.
