# EcoR Pro Community Access - Next Steps

Your Pro purchase includes private WhatsApp community access.

## How Access Works

1. Payment is confirmed.
2. You receive the download package by email.
3. The WhatsApp invite is sent manually by email.
4. If you prefer not to join WhatsApp, you can stay on email-only support.

## Community Boundary

- use the group for EcoR workflow questions
- do not expect instant replies
- do not post sensitive or confidential datasets
- do not treat the group as unlimited consulting

If anything about access is missing, reply directly to the delivery email.
