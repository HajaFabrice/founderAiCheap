# OPLURIX Fulfillment SOP

Purpose: fulfill paid product deliveries consistently in English or French.

## Current fulfillment truth

- Product 1 is the only live product now.
- French delivery packs exist for Products 1 to 4.
- Delivery is manual unless a platform-specific automation is later added.

## Before sending anything

1. Confirm which product was bought.
2. Confirm which language the buyer expects.
3. Confirm which asset version is current.
4. Confirm whether any support window or bonus was promised.

### Step 2. Check the support boundary

Before sending, read the support note in the package. Do not imply:

- custom consulting
- unlimited revisions
- automatic institutional signoff

### Step 3. Send the right files

Minimum:

- the correct package or final ZIP
- one short delivery email
- one sentence on what the product is for
- one sentence on what it does not include
