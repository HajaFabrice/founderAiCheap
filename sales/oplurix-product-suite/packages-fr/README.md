# OPLURIX French Delivery Packages

This folder contains the first French-language delivery variants for the
launch-ready OPLURIX products.

Current scope:

- Product 1: Expert-to-Influencer Content Engine
- Product 2: Training-to-Quiz Generator
- Product 3: Gear & Equipment Concierge
- Product 4: Biodiversity Pitch Deck Builder

Deferred for a later wave:

- Products 5 to 10

Reason:

- the underlying delivery assets for those products are still less stable than
  Products 1 to 4, so translating them now would create more rework than value

Important notes:

- The English packages in `sales/oplurix-product-suite/packages/` remain the
  source reference and are untouched.
- These French packages are founder-controlled delivery bundles, not automatic
  storefront activations.
- Products 2 and 4 still require the same launch approvals as their English
  counterparts before public checkout goes live.
- Product 3 is translated as a founder-controlled source package, but it stays
  held/source-only until the equipment facts, market pricing, and update
  workflow are refreshed.
- Where the original package wrappers contained unresolved scaffolding, the
  French packages use cleaned manual files instead.

Package map:

- `01-expert-to-influencer-content-engine-fr`
- `02-training-to-quiz-generator-fr`
- `03-gear-equipment-concierge-fr`
- `04-biodiversity-pitch-deck-builder-fr`
