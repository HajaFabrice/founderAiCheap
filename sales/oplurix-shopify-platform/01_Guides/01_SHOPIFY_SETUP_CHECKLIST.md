# Shopify Setup Checklist

## Store Foundations

- Create the Shopify store under the OPLURIX brand
- Set store currency to `USD`
- Set public contact email to the OPLURIX address you want buyers to use
- Add your legal business details and billing settings
- Configure your custom domain if you want Shopify to replace or complement the current storefront

## Theme and Navigation

- Choose a simple theme first; optimize clarity before design flourishes
- Create a homepage section for:
  - mission
  - featured products
  - live now
  - coming next
  - contact / institutional inquiry
- Create navigation items for:
  - Home
  - Shop
  - About OPLURIX
  - Contact
  - Institutional / Custom Inquiry

## Essential Pages

- About
- Contact
- Refund / support boundary
- Privacy policy
- Terms of service
- Digital delivery policy

## Apps / Platform Setup

- Install a digital delivery app before activating downloadable products
- Good categories to look for:
  - digital downloads
  - file delivery
  - secure download links
- Confirm the app can:
  - attach files to products or variants
  - support ZIP delivery
  - support customer access after purchase

## Product Import

- Import `02_Shopify_CSV/shopify_product_import_draft.csv`
- Confirm products land in `draft` status
- Review handles, titles, pricing, and descriptions
- Create collections from `02_Shopify_CSV/shopify_collections_plan.csv`

## Media Upload

- Upload product PDFs from `03_Upload_Files/Product_PDFs/` as supporting files or references
- Upload ZIPs from `03_Upload_Files/Digital_Delivery_ZIPs/` only after the delivery app is ready
- Create or source real product cover images before public launch

## Testing

- Test one low-risk product end to end first
- Prefer `Expert-to-Influencer Content Engine` as the first candidate
- Confirm:
  - checkout works
  - payment confirmation works
  - download access works
  - email and support pathways are visible

## Activation Order

1. Expert-to-Influencer Content Engine
2. EcoR Starter and EcoR Complete after delivery review
3. EcoR Pro Bundle only after support workflow review
4. Training-to-Quiz and Biodiversity Pitch Deck after packaging review
5. Institutional and source-only products only after explicit founder approval
