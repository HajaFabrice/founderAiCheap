# Product Upload And Activation Guide

## Why The Import Starts In Draft

Shopify can make it easy to publish faster than your operations are ready.

This pack prevents that by default:

- import first
- review second
- activate third

## Product Status Guidance

### Closest To Activation

- `Expert-to-Influencer Content Engine`

### Draft First, Then Validate Delivery

- `EcoR Toolkit Starter`
- `EcoR Toolkit Complete`
- `EcoR Toolkit Pro Bundle`

### Keep Contact-Led Or Manual

- `EcoR Toolkit Institutional`

### Keep As Draft / Coming Soon

- `Training-to-Quiz Generator`
- `Biodiversity Pitch Deck Builder`

### Keep Off Checkout Until Promoted

- Gear & Equipment Concierge
- Handwritten Log Digitizer
- Field Mission Planner
- Biodiversity Data Harmonizer
- Thermal Data Translator
- Grant Writing Co-Pilot
- MRV Report Architect

## Variant / Delivery Notes

- EcoR is safer as separate products or tightly controlled variants because delivery files differ by tier
- Institutional should not be treated as ordinary instant-download checkout
- Pro Bundle should not imply support you cannot yet deliver reliably

## Pre-Activation Checks Per Product

- Title and handle look clean
- Price matches current truth
- Description does not overpromise
- Delivery file is attached to the correct product
- Refund / support boundary is explicit
- Contact path is visible

## Suggested First Live Product

Use `Expert-to-Influencer Content Engine` as the first full Shopify test because:

- it is already live elsewhere
- the promise is clear
- the buyer file is simple
- support complexity is lower than EcoR
