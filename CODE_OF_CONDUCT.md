# Code Of Conduct

FounderAI-Ollama-Rust is a mission-driven project built to support serious work.
We want the repo to feel calm, rigorous, and welcoming.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct
- Assume good intent, but stay honest about risks and flaws
- Explain decisions clearly, especially when rejecting an idea or change
- Help newcomers get oriented instead of gatekeeping
- Keep debate focused on the work, not the person

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks or repeated bad-faith escalation
- Dismissing contributors for asking basic questions
- Sharing private credentials, personal data, or unsafe instructions
- Pressuring maintainers to bypass approvals or safety constraints

## Project-Specific Norms

- Founder voice, approvals, and protected actions are not optional details
- Safety concerns should be raised early and documented plainly
- Changes that weaken auditability or approval gating may be declined even if they seem convenient

## Reporting

Open a private maintainer contact if the issue is sensitive.
Otherwise, open an issue describing the behavior and context.

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject content that violates this code of conduct.
