# Independent Freelance Templates

Updated: 2026-04-27

Use these templates only for clearly independent, non-Techni work.

Before drafting:
- classify the lead as `independent`, `employer`, or `ambiguous`
- if the lead is employer-owned or ambiguous, stop and use
  `NEEDS_HUMAN_CLARIFICATION`
- do not mention Techni-Drones in these templates
- do not pitch ERIS in first-cycle independent outreach

## Positioning Lines

English:

- `I help conservation teams clean, validate, and structure biodiversity datasets so they are ready for analysis, mapping, and donor reporting.`
- `I support NGOs and researchers with fast, traceable cleanup of field, GPS, camera-trap, and monitoring datasets.`

French:

- `J'aide les equipes de conservation a nettoyer, valider et structurer leurs jeux de donnees de biodiversite pour l'analyse, la cartographie et le reporting bailleurs.`
- `Je propose un support rapide et traceable pour le nettoyage de donnees de terrain, GPS, camera-trap et suivi environnemental.`

## Template 1: Independent Sample Review Outreach

Subject options:

- `Quick review for [ORG]'s monitoring dataset`
- `Small data-cleanup support for [ORG]`

Body:

```text
Dear [Name / Team],

I came across [programme / report / dataset context] and thought a very small, practical offer might be useful.

I help conservation teams clean and validate biodiversity datasets so they are easier to use for analysis, mapping, and donor reporting. If useful, I can review a small sample of 5-20 rows and return three concrete fixes at no cost.

If the sample is useful, I can then propose a short, bounded scope for cleanup or reporting support.

Would you like me to review a small sample?

Best regards,
[Name]
```

Rules:

- One CTA only.
- No pricing in the first message.
- Mention one concrete deliverable.
- If personalization is missing, add a short `Verification:` block instead of guessing.

## Template 2: Positive Reply To Interest

```text
Hi [Name],

Thank you. Please send a small sample export and, if possible:
- the context of the dataset
- the variables that matter most
- the output you need most urgently

I will review the sample and return a short note covering the likely fixes, the questions that still need clarification, and the best next step for a bounded scope.

Best regards,
[Name]
```

## Template 4: Delivery Summary Note

```text
Completed work:
- Standardized dates and field formatting
- Harmonized species or variable naming where needed
- Flagged or corrected suspect coordinates where possible
- Removed duplicates or inconsistent rows where justified
- Added validation and correction notes for traceability

What to check:
- Any rows marked for human review
- Any missing values that could not be inferred safely
- Whether you want a second pass for reporting or mapping outputs
```
