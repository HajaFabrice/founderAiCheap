## Core Positioning Lines

Use one of these when a short service statement is needed:

- `I help conservation researchers and NGOs turn field and camera-trap data from Madagascar into clear analyses, figures, and short reports ready for grants or publication, using R and AI-assisted workflows.`
- `Conservation data analyst specializing in camera-trap and field survey data, delivering fast, publication-ready summaries and figures using R and AI-assisted tools.`

## Template 1: Conservation Drone-Service Outreach

Use for Techni-Drones conservation and biodiversity outreach. Keep it short and
lead with the free demonstration-flight offer.

Subject options:

- `Free demonstration flight for [site/programme]`
- `Drone support for [ORG]'s biodiversity monitoring`

Body:

```text
Dear [Name / Team],

I read [specific report, programme, or monitoring page] and was struck by [one true observation].

I work at Techni-Drones Madagascar on conservation and monitoring applications. We are building practical support for biodiversity surveys, habitat mapping, patrol-area coverage, and MRV-oriented field workflows.

If useful, I can propose a free demonstration flight for [their site / study area] so your team can see what thermal, multispectral, or mapping outputs would actually look like in your context.

Would a short call or a brief concept note be more useful?

Best regards,
[Name]
```

Rules:

- No pricing in the first message.
- One CTA only.
- Do not promise equipment or permissions that are not already secured.

## Template 2: Biodiversity Data Support Outreach

Use when the entry offer is a sample data review rather than a flight.

Subject options:

- `Support for [dataset/programme] data`
- `Quick help for [ORG]'s monitoring data`

Body:

```text
Dear [Name / Team],

I read [specific report, programme, or monitoring page] and was impressed by [one real observation].

I help conservation teams turn messy field exports, camera-trap tables, GPS records, and survey spreadsheets into analysis-ready datasets with clear validation notes and short reporting summaries.

If useful, I can review a very small sample (5-20 rows) and send three practical fixes at no cost. If the sample is useful, I can then propose a short, bounded cleaning or analysis scope.

Would you like me to review a small sample?

Best regards,
[Name]
```

Rules:

- Mention one concrete deliverable, not a vague capability list.
- Keep the CTA binary and low-friction.

## Template 5: Positive Reply To Sample Request

```text
Hi [Name],

Thank you. Please send a small sample export and, if possible:
- the field or programme name
- the variables you care most about
- the output you ultimately need (clean dataset, map-ready export, summary note, figure, or other)

I will review the sample and return a short note with the fixes I would apply, the likely turnaround, and any questions that need clarification.

Best regards,
[Name]
```

## Template 6: Post-Sample Quote Reply

```text
Hi [Name],

Based on the sample, I estimate this scope as:
- Deliverable: [clean dataset / validation note / summary / figures]
- Turnaround: [X days]
- Price: NEEDS_HUMAN_VERIFICATION

I can keep the scope narrow and deliver only the items that are immediately useful for your reporting, mapping, or analysis.

Best regards,
[Name]
```

## External Communication Reminders

- Keep the tone warm, scientific, and specific.
- Use French where the relationship and organization context clearly call for it.
- Treat contact emails from old draft docs as candidates until verified.
- Never send automatically. Approval stays in the loop.
