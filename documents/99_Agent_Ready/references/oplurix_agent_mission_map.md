# OPLURIX Agent Mission Map

Updated: 2026-05-07

This reference tells the agents how the OPLURIX products support the broader
FounderAI mission without drifting into fake checkout, fake delivery, or
product sprawl.

## Core Role Of The Products

- OPLURIX products are low-friction revenue and trust-building assets that help
  fund the longer ERIS mission.
- They also act as proof that Fabrice can package real conservation workflows
  into bounded, useful deliverables.
- The products should help the agents open doors, answer buyer questions,
  package value clearly, and learn from real market feedback.

## Product-State Rules

- `live`:
  - may be sold directly
  - may be used as a real CTA in outreach and replies
- `coming_soon`:
  - may be discussed publicly
  - may be used for waitlist or early-interest capture
  - must not be described as active checkout
- `contact_ready`:
  - may be discussed publicly
  - may be used as a real CTA for manual quote or onboarding conversations
  - must not be described as instant checkout
- `source_only`:
  - internal pipeline only
  - do not present as purchasable unless the founder explicitly activates it

## Product Vs Service Decision Rule

- Use a product-first path when the buyer pain is standardized, narrow, and the
  current package already fits the need.
- Use a service-first path when the work is custom, institution-specific,
  higher-stakes, or depends on human interpretation.
- Use a waitlist path when the product fit is real but the package is not yet
  activated for checkout.

## Delivery Rule

- OPLURIX currently sells through HTML pages and delivers through bounded
  package files.
- The sellable pages live in:
  - `docs/products/`
- The deliverable bundles live in:
  - `sales/oplurix-product-suite/packages/`
- ZIP previews live in:
  - `sales/oplurix-product-suite/zips/`
- Manual fulfillment and truthful delivery boundaries remain the default.

## Hard Stops

- Do not mix Techni-Drones employer leads with OPLURIX product selling.
- Do not sell source-only products.
- Do not imply automatic delivery if the real workflow is manual.
- Do not present waitlist products as instantly deliverable.
- Do not present `EcoR Toolkit Institutional` as instant checkout.
- Do not claim traction, downloads, or buyer success that is not grounded in
  the logs.
