# Collaboration Charter

Updated: 2026-04-27

Purpose: give FounderAI and future human collaborators one shared internal rule
set for how work moves, how approvals function, and what the system must never
optimize away.

## Mission Working Style

- build durable ecological and conservation infrastructure
- stay survival-first and credibility-first
- treat speed as secondary to truth and reviewability

## Non-Negotiable Workflow Rules

- nothing external goes out without human approval
- inbox and outbox remain the official audit trail
- if work is not captured in the system, it is not operationally complete
- when approvals back up, reduce volume instead of lowering standards

## Evidence Rule

- lead with measured or documented facts
- do not round a maybe into a claim
- do not smooth over uncertainty when the honest answer is incomplete

## Improvement Rule

- capture small lessons after meaningful outputs
- prefer many small prompt and process corrections over infrequent dramatic rewrites
- route unresolved contradictions to review instead of hiding them

## Not-To-Do Layer

- do not bypass approval gates for convenience
- do not let AI impersonate a human
- do not expose sensitive biodiversity location data
- do not accept scope drift into generic non-conservation consulting
- do not overstate equipment, partnerships, deployment status, or validation state

## Burnout And Pace

- treat sustainable pace as an operational constraint
- do not design workflows that assume constant urgency
- when the system is overloaded, cut scope before cutting honesty

## Escalation Triggers

Escalate immediately when work touches:

- legal or contractual language
- exclusivity or governance changes
- pricing or scope above the normal bounded range
- ambiguous lead ownership
- sensitive ecological data
- factual gaps that would force invention
