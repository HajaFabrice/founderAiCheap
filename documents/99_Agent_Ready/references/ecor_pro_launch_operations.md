# EcoR Pro Launch Operations

Updated: 2026-05-07

Use this reference when an agent needs to talk about `EcoR Toolkit Pro Bundle`.

## Current Truth

- Pro is now a live paid tier.
- Payment happens through PayPal.
- Delivery remains manual.
- Post-purchase handling includes:
  - delivery email
  - support-window activation
  - manual WhatsApp invite

## What Pro Includes

- everything in Complete
- 5 annotated R notebooks
- statistical test decision tree
- 1 month email Q&A
- private WhatsApp community access

## What Pro Does Not Promise

- no video course promise
- no unlimited consulting
- no guaranteed analytical or publication outcome

## Agent Rule

- You may present Pro as a real live tier.
- You must keep the support boundary explicit.
- If the buyer sounds like a team rather than an individual, route toward the institutional path instead.
