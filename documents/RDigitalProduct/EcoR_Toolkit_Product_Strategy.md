# EcoR Toolkit Product Strategy

Updated: 2026-05-04

This file is the synced public-truth strategy for EcoR Toolkit. It replaces the
older launch assumptions that used the original `$19 / $39 / $69` ladder.

## Product Identity

- Name: `EcoR Toolkit`
- Positioning:
  - a practical biodiversity analysis toolkit built by a field biologist
  - helps buyers move from messy field data to cleaner R workflows, figures,
    and Results-ready outputs faster
- Format:
  - digital bundle
  - scripts
  - templates
  - prompt library
  - workflow guides

## Public Pricing Ladder

### Tier 1 — Starter (`USD 29`)

- Role:
  - entry tier
- Best for:
  - buyers who want the core scripts, prompts, and quick-start assets
- Includes:
  - 10 annotated R scripts
  - 50 Claude prompts for debugging and interpretation
  - quick-start guide
- Public truth:
  - this is the low-friction entry point

### Tier 2 — Complete (`USD 97`)

- Role:
  - main offer
  - first-sale focus tier
- Best for:
  - most buyers
  - especially ecology or conservation students and early-career researchers
- Includes:
  - everything in Starter
  - 5 field data templates
  - 3 ggplot2 figure templates
  - workflow guide from field notes to Results section
- Public truth:
  - this is the tier the storefront should push most often

### Tier 3 — Pro Bundle (`USD 149`)

- Role:
  - public anchor tier
- Planned extras:
  - video walkthroughs
  - community access
  - one month of email Q&A support
- Public truth:
  - do not take payment for this tier until the walkthrough assets and support
    workflow are actually ready
  - keep it visible as an anchor or request-access tier only

## Delivery Truth

- Frontend:
  - Netlify storefront
- Checkout:
  - PayPal
- Delivery mode:
  - manual email delivery after payment confirmation during the first sales
    cycle
- Delivery window target:
  - within `12 hours` after payment confirmation

## Positioning

### The gap EcoR fills

Most R tutorials teach syntax. Most generic AI tools do not understand
biodiversity workflows. EcoR is useful because it helps a buyer move through the
real sequence:

- raw field notes or CSV files
- data cleaning and structure
- analysis setup
- tables and figures
- Results-ready outputs

### Founder advantage

- real Madagascar field experience
- biodiversity data handling grounded in real surveys and research
- co-author credit in `Flowers et al. (2023)`
- credibility with the exact audience the product serves

## First-Sale Guidance

- Primary sales target:
  - `EcoR Toolkit Complete`
- If the buyer feels price friction:
  - offer `Starter`
- Do not discount `Complete` immediately
- Do not actively sell `Pro Bundle` until the promised assets are live

## Public Claims To Keep

- built by a field biologist
- designed for biodiversity data workflows
- helps reduce setup time and blank-page friction
- does not replace statistical judgment

## Public Claims To Avoid

- do not say the toolkit guarantees publication
- do not say it replaces ecological or statistical expertise
- do not say delivery is instant if delivery is still manual
- do not imply support assets exist if they are not packaged yet
