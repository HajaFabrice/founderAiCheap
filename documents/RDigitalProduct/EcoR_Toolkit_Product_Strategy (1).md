## Commercial Rule

- `Complete` is the main offer and first-sale focus tier.
- `Starter` is the lower-friction entry tier.
- `Pro Bundle` is a public anchor only until its promised walkthrough assets and
  support workflow are truly ready.

## Delivery Rule

- Checkout happens through PayPal.
- Delivery is still manual during the first sales cycle.
- Buyer expectation:
  - delivery follows by email within `12 hours` after payment confirmation.

## Audience

- ecology and biology graduate students
- early-career researchers
- field biologists
- conservation researchers working with biodiversity count data

## Core Promise

Help buyers move from messy biodiversity field data to cleaner R workflows,
better figures, and Results-ready outputs faster.
