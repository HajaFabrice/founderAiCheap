## How to Read This Roadmap

This document is organised in three layers:

- **Daily rhythm** — the recurring non-negotiable schedule that happens every week regardless of what else is happening
- **Weekly sprints** — what the single most important focus is for each specific week
- **Monthly milestones** — what must be true at the end of each month to be on track

The **five tracks** run in parallel throughout:
- **A** — Digital Products & Audience
- **B** — Techni-Drones Conservation Vertical
- **C** — Grant Applications
- **D** — PhD Applications
- **E** — ERIS Development

A sixth track appears from Month 4:
- **F** — MRV Consulting Pipeline
