## Positioning

EcoR Toolkit is not a generic R course. It is a practical biodiversity analysis
workflow toolkit built by a field biologist who understands the pain of moving
from messy field data to usable figures, tables, and Results-ready outputs.

## Buyer Truth

- useful for ecology and conservation buyers who already have data
- useful for early-career researchers who need cleaner workflow structure
- not a replacement for statistical judgment
- not sold as instant automated delivery while manual fulfillment is still in
  use
