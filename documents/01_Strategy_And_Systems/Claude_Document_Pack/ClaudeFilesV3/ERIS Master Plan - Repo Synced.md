# ERIS Master Plan

Repo-synced operational mirror of `01_MasterPlan_v3.docx`.

Updated: 2026-04-14

## Mission

Build measurement and governance infrastructure that redirects economic incentives toward ecological stewardship, especially for Franciscan and conservation-aligned institutions, without building a founder-accumulation machine.

## 15-Year Arc

| Phase | Timeframe | Objective | Milestone |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Foundation | Now to 18 months | Survive, learn, build credibility | First paid client in 30 days |
| MRV Startup | Years 2 to 5 | Produce decision-grade ecological data | First institutional MRV contract |
| ERIS | Years 5 to 15 | Establish a governance and measurement standard | One national or institutional MRV adoption |

## Strategic Core

- The freelance engine is not a distraction. It is the legitimacy and survival layer.
- The founder-brain is the moat: retained patterns, cleaned-data learning, and field-grounded knowledge.
- The system must grow through workflow discipline, not speculative abstraction.
- Governance is not deferred until success. It is designed in from the beginning.

## Agent-System Role in the Plan

- FounderAI becomes the operating layer that turns the strategy into inspectable daily work.
- Francis and Clare act as the interim mentor pair until a human mentor is found.
- Columban improves the system only through bounded, auditable changes.

## Non-Negotiables

- No autonomous external send.
- Transparent AI signatures for client-facing drafts.
- No work that supports extractive land use.
- No generic off-mission consulting drift.
- No skipping the 06:00 formation rhythm as a productivity hack.

## Phase 1 Priorities

1. Keep income and outreach alive.
2. Build credibility through useful service delivery.
3. Create the ERIS knowledge base and operating rules inside `founder-brain/`.
4. Maintain weekly review, QA gating, and approval discipline.
5. Accumulate reusable patterns instead of relying on memory or hype.

## Risk Logic

Primary early risks include:

- no income
- no supervisor or research foothold
- mission drift
- burnout
- unsafe automation
- connectivity instability

These should be surfaced in weekly review artifacts and daily briefings rather than treated as background noise.

## Canonical Repo Implications

- `founder-brain/forbidden_patterns.txt` defines automatic QA failures.
- `founder-brain/eris_notebook.json` is the retained learning moat.
- KPI, risk, and roadmap files should stay readable and auditable.
