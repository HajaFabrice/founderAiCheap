# KPI Thresholds

Use these thresholds to decide whether to keep course, adjust, or escalate.

| KPI | Green | Yellow | Red | Action |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Inbound first-response speed | under 30 min | 30-120 min | over 120 min | Red triggers Zacchaeus and Founder review. |
| Outreach personalization quality | over 90% pass | 75-90% | under 75% | Red triggers prompt review and QA tightening. |
| Reply rate | over 5% | 2-5% | under 2% | Red triggers messaging and targeting adjustment. |
| QA pass rate | over 90% | 80-90% | under 80% | Red triggers Clare escalation and draft review. |
| Weekly review cadence | every week | late once | skipped twice | Red triggers Francis escalation. |
| Offline queue backlog | 0-3 items | 4-10 items | over 10 items | Red triggers connectivity and replay review. |
| Approval turnaround | under 24h | 24-72h | over 72h | Red means protected work is stalling. |

## Interpretation Rules

- Green means continue and compound.
- Yellow means adjust a workflow, prompt, or target list.
- Red means stop treating it as normal noise and surface it explicitly in the weekly review.
