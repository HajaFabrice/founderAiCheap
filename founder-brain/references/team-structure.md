# Team Structure

## Core Six Lanes

FounderAI still runs through the same six operational lanes. These stay sacred.

### Team A - Survival and Immediate Cash Flow

- `A-Outreach` / Anthony: conservation NGO and drone-services outreach, free demo-flight offers, and low-friction first contact.
- `A-Production` / Juniper: delivery packets, data handling, templates, and bounded client-ready assets.

### Team B - MRV and Consulting Growth

- `B-Outreach` / Bonaventure: institutional outreach, MRV partnerships, donor-facing prospect qualification, and alliance-building.
- `B-Production` / Bernardine: proposals, budgets, MRV methods, and consulting collateral.

### Team C - PhD and Research Leverage

- `C-Outreach` / Jacinta: supervisor, grant, and research collaboration outreach.
- `C-Production` / Duns Scotus: literature synthesis, proposal sections, methods notes, and evidence-grounded research drafting.

## Overlay Workflow Agents

These do not replace the six lanes. They support them.

- Bartholomew: grant writer for Rufford, WWF Russell Train, GEF-style opportunities, and equipment narratives.
- Pio: scheduler and deadline tracker for grant, equity, and outreach milestones.
- Zacchaeus: inbound lead responder with speed-to-lead discipline.
- Perpetua: nurture-sequence manager for persistent follow-up.
- Hildegard: founder briefing and meeting-scribe support.
- Clare: QA and governance gate.
- Francis: weekly review and mentor-style governance summary.
- Columban: bounded self-improvement agent.

## Routing Rules

1. Default to Team A when the work can improve near-term cash flow.
2. Route conservation drone-service and MRV consulting growth work to Teams A and B depending on whether it is first-contact or production.
3. Route PhD, literature, and supervisor work to Team C.
4. Route grant drafting to Bartholomew, then back through Clare and Hildegard into approval.
5. Route deadlines through Pio, but never let Pio bypass approval or founder review.

## Daily Cadence

- 06:00: daily orchestration
- 06:30: Pio deadline scan
- 07:00: Hildegard briefing window
- Weekly: Francis review and roadmap check

## Logging Standard

Every meaningful run should remain auditable through runtime files, team logs, approvals, and run artifacts. New workflow agents extend the log trail; they do not replace it.
