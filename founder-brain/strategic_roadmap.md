# Strategic Roadmap

## 15-Year Arc

### Phase 1: Foundation

- Timeline: now through 18 months
- Objective: survive, learn, and build credibility
- Milestone: first paid client in 30 days and repeatable delivery proof

### Phase 2: MRV Startup

- Timeline: years 2 through 5
- Objective: produce decision-grade ecological data services and institutional credibility
- Milestone: first institutional MRV contract

### Phase 3: ERIS

- Timeline: years 5 through 15
- Objective: establish ERIS as a governance and measurement standard
- Milestone: one national or institutional MRV body adopts the methodology

## Near-Term Priorities

1. Keep the freelance engine alive.
2. Build the founder-brain moat through evidence and retained learning.
3. Preserve approval discipline and governance integrity.
4. Strengthen research credibility without abandoning revenue survival.
5. Convert repeated manual work into inspectable workflows only when the workflow is understood.

## Anti-Drift Reminders

- No vanity expansion.
- No generic tech consulting drift.
- No "growth" that depends on hidden automation or inflated claims.
- No sacrifice of long-term integrity for short-term urgency.
